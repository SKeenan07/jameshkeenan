James H. Keenan

August 3, 1955 - May 8, 2020

James Harry Keenan

Mingo Junction, Ohio

Life

Originally from Steubenville, Ohio, Jim attended Steubenville Catholic High School. He worked at Weirton Steel for many years and obtained two degrees from Jefferson Community College. An avid golfer and pianist, Jim shot three holes-in-one and mastered select Billy Joel songs. The most important aspect of Jim's life was family. He was a great husband, father, and doggy dad and will be greatly missed.
